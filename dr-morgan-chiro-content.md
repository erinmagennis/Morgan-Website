## Homepage / About

**Tagline:** Meet Your Doctor

Dr. Morgan is a board-certified chiropractor offering concierge services. Her practice focuses on holistic pain relief, with special accommodations for pregnant patients, those with unique health needs, and home-based appointments.

### About Dr. Morgan

She pursued chiropractic after experiencing severe migraines since age 10. Chiropractic care provided relief unavailable through conventional medicine, inspiring her career shift from neurology to chiropractic specialization.

## Services

### What is Concierge Chiropractic?

Home-based chiropractic care designed for patients with mobility limitations, busy schedules, or those preferring comfort at home.

### Conditions Treated
- Back and neck pain relief
- Headache and migraine reduction
- TMJ symptom improvement
- Muscle tension relief
- Injury recovery support
- Childbirth preparation

### Treatment Methods
Spinal adjustments, extremity work, manual therapies including myofascial release, cupping, and Graston technique, plus home exercise programs.

## Laser Therapy

**Introducing Laser Therapy**

*Navigation: Home | Office | Laser Therapy*

Low-Level Laser Therapy (LLLT) is a safe, non-invasive solution designed to relieve pain and reduce inflammation by using the power of light. This laser is FDA-cleared to help with neck pain, shoulder pain, pre-surgery, and post-surgery. However, it can help with many more conditions!

This process supports the body's natural healing system in helping tissues repair faster and reducing discomfort. LLLT is a safe and effective way to relieve pain and improve well-being.

This is a great way to relieve pain faster and more efficiently in conjunction with chiropractic care!
